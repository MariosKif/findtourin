# A Guide to Sustainable Travel

Travel broadens the mind, but it can also impact the planet. Here's how to explore responsibly.

## Choose Eco-Friendly Transport

When possible, opt for trains over flights. If flying is necessary, consider carbon offset programs.

## Support Local Businesses

Eat at local restaurants, shop at markets, and choose locally-owned accommodations. Your money goes further when it stays in the community.

## Reduce Single-Use Plastic

Carry a reusable water bottle, shopping bag, and utensils. Many destinations now have water refill stations.

## Respect Wildlife

Avoid attractions that exploit animals. Choose ethical wildlife tours that observe animals in their natural habitat.

## Stay on Marked Trails

When hiking, stick to designated paths to minimize erosion and protect native vegetation.

## Learn About Local Culture

A little effort goes a long way. Learn a few words in the local language, understand customs, and dress appropriately when visiting religious sites.
